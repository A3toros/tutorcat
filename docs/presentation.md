# TutorCat — Classroom AI English Learning Platform

## Concept
TutorCat is an AI-powered English learning platform designed for students and daily classroom use. It combines structured CEFR-aligned lessons with gamified activities and speaking practice, then gives students immediate, actionable feedback (grammar, vocabulary, fluency, and pronunciation-focused guidance) while providing teachers visibility into progress and integrity.

## Objectives
- Improve students’ speaking confidence through guided, repeatable practice.
- Provide instant, personalized AI feedback on grammar, vocabulary, and clarity.
- Support classroom workflows (individual practice, pair work, homework, teacher review).
- Track learning progress across lessons and activities with clear completion signals.
- Encourage honest learning with AI integrity / AI-detection signals (risk scoring) to reduce copy/paste or over-assisted answers.
- Make practice engaging via gamification and a friendly “TutorCat” companion experience.

## Outcomes (What students achieve)
- **Better speaking performance**: clearer sentences, fewer recurring grammar mistakes, and more natural phrasing.
- **Vocabulary growth**: more appropriate word choice for the student’s level, fewer off-level or off-topic words.
- **Higher fluency and confidence**: students practice frequently, get feedback immediately, and iterate quickly.
- **Measurable progress**: activity results and lesson completion are recorded and can be reviewed.
- **Improved self-correction**: students learn patterns of mistakes and how to fix them.

## Advantages (Why it’s strong for schools)
- **Instant AI feedback at scale**: every student can receive feedback without waiting for teacher availability.
- **Grammar-focused improvement**: identifies errors and provides corrected, level-appropriate alternatives.
- **Vocabulary guidance**: suggests better words/phrases, avoids overly advanced vocabulary, reinforces CEFR alignment.
- **Speaking practice support**: encourages repeated attempts and refinement using structured prompts.
- **AI integrity / AI-detection**: risk scoring and “flagging” help teachers spot likely AI-generated or overly assisted responses.
- **Teacher-friendly oversight**: activity results can be reviewed; supports classroom monitoring and follow-up.
- **Consistent structure**: lessons and activities create repeatable routines for class periods and homework.
- **Engagement and motivation**: gamified tasks plus a friendly brand mascot improves participation.
- **Data-driven instruction**: teachers can identify common weak points (grammar topics, vocabulary gaps) and adjust teaching.

## Key Features
### Student learning experience
- **CEFR-aligned lessons** with progressive difficulty (A1 → higher levels).
- **Interactive activities** (e.g., vocabulary tests, matching, fill-in-blanks, speaking tasks).
- **AI feedback** on:
  - **Grammar** (error detection + corrected versions)
  - **Vocabulary** (word choice, level-appropriate suggestions)
  - **Clarity and coherence** (how to express ideas more naturally)
  - **Speaking improvement guidance** (how to say it better next time)
- **Progress tracking**: saves activity results and supports lesson completion flow.

### Integrity and quality controls
- **AI-detection / integrity risk scoring** to flag suspiciously perfect or off-level answers.
- **Teacher review support**: flagged submissions can be checked quickly to encourage authentic practice.

### Classroom usage modes
- **In-class station work**: students rotate through speaking/vocab activities while teacher monitors.
- **Homework practice**: structured prompts + feedback keep practice meaningful at home.
- **Small group practice**: students compare AI feedback and rewrite answers together.
- **Assessment support**: teachers can use activity results and integrity flags as part of formative assessment.

## Summary (1 slide version)
TutorCat helps students practice English actively, receive immediate AI feedback on grammar and vocabulary, and improve speaking confidence—while giving teachers progress visibility and AI-integrity detection to support honest learning in the classroom.
